Hi, and thanks for visiting! My name is Ayanna and I am a graduate student at USM in Mississippi! This summer, (August 2021) I will be graduating with a Masters in Library & Information Science. Currently, I am the Teen Librarian for the Russell Library in Middletown, CT. I create programming and develop a collection of books and resources specifically for young people ages 12-20.

When I am not clocked in, I can be found doing a ton of other things!! My hobbies include: gardening, reading, cooking, traveling with family and friends, mentoring teens, crafting and photography! In true librarian form this site is meant to connect you to resources that may prove to be useful, so bookmark this page! I appreciate your support, please contact me for possible networking opportunities!
