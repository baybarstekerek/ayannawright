Crafting

Crafting is a great outlet for creatives! I’ll admit I don’t get to do as much of it as I would like, but as a librarian I get to create programming for young people and I often like to incorporate easy crafting into what I offer. In the midst of the pandemic, it had gotten increasingly more stressful for young people to have fun or find inside activities to cure the monotony of being in the house everyday, so virtual crafting was my way of connecting with young people as well helping myself stay focused and busy.
