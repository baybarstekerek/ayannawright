Photography

I picked up my first DSLR back in 2011 while on deployment in Guantanamo Bay. It was a Canon Rebel T1i and I bought it because there was a group advertising photography courses and I was interested in learning. With some mentoring, a little money and a lot of youtube, I learned how to take photos, edit them and learn the business aspect of it to make it a profitable hobby. Fast forward through the years, I was able to earn an undergraduate degree in Film, TV and Media Studies and find work as a Digital Media Mentor, teaching workshops about photography and other forms of digital art! Don’t believe them when they say hobbies can not become careers!
