Mentoring

My mentoring journey began back in 2017 when I began working at a digital media makerspace for teens in my city’s library. I was able to create programming with a focus on photography, video production, editing and writing for various media outlets. Mentoring in the library is what gave me the opportunity to become a librarian.



As someone who grew up in the inner city, I know the importance of mentoring and working with young people. It goes beyond giving valuable advice, mentoring can help young people develop knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their field or career of choice.
