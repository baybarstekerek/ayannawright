MY GARDEN

Gardening became a hobby of mine about 3 years ago back in 2018. I was struggling with anxiety and depression after undergrad, and found plants as a way to distract me from constantly overthinking.

After a while, I began to read that there is a correlation between gardening and mental health and certain plants have medicinal properties.

Right now I am a mom to over 25 plants, some sleep inside and others sleep outside (although this changes every few months in New England).

Being a plant parent does have its challenges and sometimes you have to deal with pests inside and out, but there is something special about growing your own produce, herbs or flowers from a seed.

    Here is a list of books that have really helped me with gardening:

  • Urban Botanics
  • Your Brain on Plants
  • The Complete Houseplant Survival Manual

Also check out this youtube tutorial, it was super helpful to me when I first started out!
